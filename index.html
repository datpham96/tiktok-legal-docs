AutoPublisher

AutoPublisher is a creator workflow platform that researches trending topics, generates vertical short-form videos with AI-written captions, and publishes content through official platform APIs after user authorization.

Product Features

Trend Research

Discover relevant topics in technology, AI, and creator-economy niches to plan content that matches audience demand.

AI Video Creation

Build multi-scene vertical videos with generated visuals, on-screen text overlays, and pacing optimized for short-form platforms.

Caption & Hashtag Generation

Produce engaging captions and hashtag sets for each topic using AI language models tuned for creator workflows.

Authorized Publishing

Connect a user account through OAuth, then publish approved content only after explicit user consent and scope authorization.

How It Works

  1. Sign in and connect your social account through the platform's official Login Kit (OAuth 2.0).
  2. AutoPublisher researches a topic and plans a multi-scene storyboard for your video.
  3. AI generates scene visuals, captions, and hashtags based on your selected topic.
  4. Review the output, then publish through the authorized API integration.

About AutoPublisher

AutoPublisher is operated as a web-based creator tool focused on helping individuals and small teams produce short-form content faster while keeping publishing actions under user control.

Our service integrates with third-party developer APIs only after users grant permission. We do not sell personal data and we provide clear legal documentation for terms of use and privacy practices.
