AutoPublisher Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. Introduction

AutoPublisher ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our content creation and publishing platform.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect a social platform account, we access only the data authorized by you through OAuth, which may include:

  • Basic profile information (user ID, display name, avatar)
  • Permissions to upload and publish videos on your behalf

We do not access your account password, private messages, or data beyond the scopes you explicitly authorize.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your connected account via OAuth 2.0
  • Publish video content you create through the Service
  • Maintain your session and refresh API access tokens

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely and are used only to interact with authorized platform APIs on your behalf. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. Tokens are not shared with third parties except as required to communicate with those APIs.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • Social platform APIs — for account authentication and content publishing.
  • AI language models — for generating captions and content ideas. No personal account data is sent to AI providers beyond topic text you choose.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

OAuth tokens are retained while your account is connected. You may disconnect your account at any time through the platform's app permission settings, which revokes our API access. You may request deletion of stored data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may revoke AutoPublisher's access to your connected account at any time through the platform's security and app permission settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at contact@autopublisher.click.
