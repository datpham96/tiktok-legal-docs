Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

AutoTikTok ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our TikTok content creation tool.

2. Information We Collect via TikTok Login Kit

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only the data authorized by you through TikTok OAuth, which may include:

  • Basic profile information (user ID, display name, avatar)
  • Permissions to upload and publish videos on your behalf

We do not access your TikTok password, private messages, or data beyond the scopes you explicitly authorize.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Publish video content you create through the Service to your TikTok account
  • Maintain your session and refresh API access tokens

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored securely on our servers and are used only to interact with the TikTok API on your behalf. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. Tokens are not shared with third parties except as required to communicate with TikTok's API.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • TikTok API — for account authentication and content publishing. See TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • AI language models — for generating captions and content ideas. No personal TikTok data is sent to AI providers beyond topic text you choose.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

OAuth tokens are retained while your account is connected. You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings, which revokes our API access. You may request deletion of stored data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may revoke AutoTikTok's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok Settings > Security > Manage app permissions.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age, in accordance with TikTok's minimum age requirements.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at contact@autotiktok.click.
